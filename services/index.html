QUICKSTART

You mean... I can monetize unique assets directly in Discord?
Yes. That's where your community is...
...so let's get started!

To install the plugin, first connect your Discord server. The plugin will be installed to your integrations tab in your Discord server settings and you'll immediately be able to start publishing assets/items/NFTs for your users to trade. Our tools will walk you through how to create NFTs for sale only in Discord, connect those assets to your game via tokenized APIs, teach your users how to watch, buy, and trade those assets within your Discord community and much more. Take full control over your in-game economy and protect your users' hard-earned collectables with our integrated Discord Inventory Wallet.

  • Latest release v1.8.9
